RETURNS & REFUNDS

Returns & Refunds

At Aurella, your satisfaction is our priority. If you’re not completely happy with your purchase, we’re here to make the process as easy as possible.

1. Eligibility for Returns

  • Products can be returned within 7 days of delivery.
  • Items must be unused, unopened, and in their original packaging.
  • Proof of purchase (invoice or order confirmation) is required.

2. Non-Returnable Items

For hygiene and safety reasons, the following cannot be returned:

  • Opened or used beauty products
  • Items with broken seals or damaged packaging
  • Products purchased during clearance or final sale

3. Refund Process

  • Once we receive and inspect the returned item, we will process your refund.
  • Refunds will be made to your original payment method within 7–10 business days.
  • Shipping charges are non-refundable unless the product is defective or incorrect.

4. Exchanges

  • Exchanges are available for defective or wrongly delivered products.
  • If the desired item is unavailable, we will issue a full refund.

5. Damaged or Defective Items

  • If you receive a damaged or defective product, please contact us within 48 hours of delivery with photos or videos as proof.
  • We will arrange a replacement at no extra cost.

6. How to Initiate a Return

You have the right to:

  • Email us at info@aurella.com or call +1 234-456-7890 with your order details.
  • Our team will guide you through the return process.
