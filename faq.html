FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Are your beauty products safe for all skin types?

Yes. All our products are dermatologically tested and free from harmful chemicals like parabens, sulfates, and mineral oils. However, we always recommend doing a patch test before first use, especially if you have sensitive skin.

Q: Are your products cruelty-free?

Absolutely. We are proudly cruelty-free and never test our products on animals.

Q: How can I find the right product for my skin or hair?

You can use our Skin & Hair Quiz or connect with our beauty experts for a free online consultation to get personalized recommendations.

Q: Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship worldwide. Shipping charges and delivery times vary based on your location and will be displayed at checkout.

Q: How long will it take for my order to arrive?

Orders are usually processed within 24–48 hours. Delivery typically takes 3–5 business days within India and 7–14 business days for international orders.

Q: What is your return policy?

We offer easy returns within 7 days of delivery for unopened and unused products. Please refer to our Return Policy page for detailed instructions.

Q: Can I get free samples before buying?

Yes! We include complimentary samples with eligible orders so you can try new products risk-free.
